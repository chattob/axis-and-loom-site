# Axis and Loom

Axis and Loom is built on the idea that a pedal can be more than a utility effect. It should feel like a compact instrument: immediate to touch, expressive under pressure, and capable of moving between control and instability without losing its musical center.

## Philosophy

The project favors playable DSP over feature accumulation. Each control should open a clear musical gesture, not send the player into menu-diving or parameter management. The goal is to make experimentation physical and intuitive, so texture, motion, and degradation stay connected to performance.

Looping, pitch shifting, reverb, delay, distortion, and crushing are treated as one continuous surface rather than isolated effects. Clean tones, damaged tones, stretched tones, and unstable harmonies should all feel like related states of the same instrument.

The name reflects the balance at the core of the design. **Axis** is structure, timing, and repeatable intent. **Loom** is layering, memory, blur, and emergence. The pedal lives in the tension between those two forces.
